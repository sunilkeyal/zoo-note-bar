Three design directions

Each applies all your improvements but with a different philosophy. Which resonates?

A

Clean & Minimal

Notion / Google Docs vibe

White space, subtle borders, thin header, clean sidebar with search, toolbar as a compact strip. Balanced and approachable.

Pros

  • Familiar to most users
  • Low visual clutter
  • Works in both themes

Cons

  • Less distinctive
  • Could feel generic
B

Modern & Structured

Linear / Obsidian vibe

Bolder use of color, subtle glassmorphism, sidebar with note preview lines, collapsible sections, floating toolbar. More opinionated.

Pros

  • Distinctive look
  • Efficient use of space
  • Great dark mode

Cons

  • Heavier visual style
  • More CSS complexity
C

Distraction-Free

Bear / iA Writer vibe

Sidebar is a slide-over panel (not permanent), toolbar collapses contextually, maximum focus on the writing area, beautiful typography.

Pros

  • Best writing experience
  • Unique positioning
  • Great for focus

Cons

  • Less discoverable UI
  • Takes getting used to
